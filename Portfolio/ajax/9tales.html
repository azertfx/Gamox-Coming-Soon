9tales NFT Card Game

Project Info:

The 9Tales is a blockchain-based play-to-earn card game that allows players to earn real money through their in-game NFT ownership. The word of 9Tales has 9 races: the Elves, the Elementals, the Old ones, the Angels, the Humans, the Orcs, the Beastmen, the Moutas, and the Djinns.
