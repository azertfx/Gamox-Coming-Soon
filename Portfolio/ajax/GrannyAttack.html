Granny Attack

Project Info:

You are a boy with strange behavior who went to visit his grandma and grandpa, you have to annoy them and throw things at them, they are mad at you, they will run after you to catch you and kick you, and throw household things at you, you are fast and they are slow, you can dodge things and attack them.
