Save Colors Carnival

Project Info:

A monster stole all colors of a carnival, and you have to get all colors an collect them in the right order, one mistake and you will caught by the monster, you can walk and jump, but the monster shouldn't see you, he is so smart and fast, so be carful and hide behind the tents and wait for the perfect time to walk to the next color.
