Solo Levelling

Project Info:

The ultimate solo levelling fight against 2 monsters, you are inside a cave and there is 2 monsters trying to chase you, and you have no weapon only the gold items that you can find on the floor, you can pick it and throw it towards the monsters, but be carful because the monster doing same too, so you have to stay in full focus.
