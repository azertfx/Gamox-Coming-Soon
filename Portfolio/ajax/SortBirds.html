Sort Birds

Project Info:

A puzzle game where you have to sort the birds according to their type and color. There are 4 different levels, and each level contains many stages. Focus on every move you make, be smart and think 3 or 4 steps ahead of you. If you feel that you made a wrong move, you have the right to go back.
