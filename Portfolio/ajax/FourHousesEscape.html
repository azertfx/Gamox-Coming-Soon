Four Houses Escape

Project Info:

A game where you have to look in 4 houses for the right item depends on the task given to you, and you should be fast or your heart will go down to 0, every task have a hint to guid you to find the right item, move fast and remember all items places because you will need it later, and last item is the key to escape this houses.

Project Details:

  • Position: Unity Game Developer
  • Tasks:
