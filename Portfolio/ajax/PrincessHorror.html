Princess Horror

Project Info:

In the heart of the evil forest, you are on a mission to rescue a captive princess who is imprisoned in a mysterious prison, you must find all the necessary items in that dark forest to rescue her, but be careful, she will try to chase you immediately, so you have to know the shortest direct path to the gate to get out and win.
